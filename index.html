Discovery Phase: Project Analysis

Site Intent

The redesigned Spring Lake Elementary School should be aesthetically pleasing, user friendly and informative while coinciding with their mission statement of working in partnership with its families and community, and to strive for excellence by providing a learning environment that will enable all students to achieve their potential. This can be achieved by providing quick and easy access for the user to find information about the school's contact information, location, policies and procedures, an academic calendar, and an easy to use contact form to be used for contacting the school with any questions or concerns.

In order to expresses what the school overall goals and objectives are for the current year, the site should focus on:

  • Creative - To focus on the aesthetically pleasing aspect, the site needs to have a creative and fun way to for the user to gather information. The website will create a user experience that will engage the audience.
  • Dynamic - In order to truly know the target audience, it is necessary to be able to capture and collect as much data as possible on how much traffic the site receives and the areas of inquiry.
  • Informative - The website will keep everyone updated in real-time of emergencies, events, closures and other pertinent information.
  • Strategic - In order to capture the attention of parents, students and other visitors to the site, targeted messages and graphics will be prominently displayed across the site.

Competitive Analysis

Research reveals that users searching for schools in the Altamonte Springs, Florida area are searching for schools by city and county. Therefore adding keywords such as "Seminole County Public Schools", "Altamonte Springs schools", and "schools in Altamonte Springs FL" should be added to the site while performing (SEO) Search Engine Optimization on the site. Another common search phrase was "compare and contrast". This suggests that users are looking for the best schools in the area. In order to capture potential viewers, it would be beneficial to include that search phrase as well as "best schools in Altamonte Springs FL" or "top schools in Altamonte Springs FL".

As one of 12 elementary schools serving the Altamonte Springs area in Seminole County Florida, Spring Lake Elementary School is a K-5th grade school in the Seminole County Public Schools District. Based on its state standardized test performance, Spring Lake has received a Great Schools Rating of 6 out of 10. 31 members of the community have posted reviews, giving the school an average Community Rating of 4 out of 5 stars.

Lack of communication has been the biggest complaint about Spring Lake Elementary School according to some recent reviews. In order for the school and its teachers to positively communicate in a more open and effective manner with parents and students, the website and other online tools should be used to its full potential.

Targeted Audience

The target audience searching for elementary schools in the Altamonte Springs Florida are primarily the mothers who are searching online from home, with some college education, that are in the age range of 35-44, followed by ages 45-54. These are families who have children entering into Kindergarten and up through 5th grade. Part of this initiative will be focusing on businesses and other community organizations interested in supporting the county public school objectives and goals.
